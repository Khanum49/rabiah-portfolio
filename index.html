About Me

I’m Rabiah, a Technical Writer and Electrical Engineer based in Islamabad, Pakistan. I specialize in building modern documentation systems for SaaS and cloud platforms, with a strong focus on clarity, scalability, and maintainability.

At my current role, Technical Writer at Xgrid, I work on end-to-end documentation workflows, from designing Docs-as-Code pipelines using Python and GitHub Actions to writing and maintaining high-quality technical documentation with Sphinx and reStructuredText.

My background in Electrical Engineering, combined with my experience as a Harvard Aspire Leaders Program alumna, allows me to bridge the gap between complex systems and clear communication.

Outside of work, I practice Arabic and English calligraphy and enjoy long-form storytelling through reading and writing, interests that deeply influence how I think about structure, precision, and narrative in technical documentation.

If you’d like to talk documentation, systems, or books, feel free to reach out on LinkedIn.
